1) Covid-19 detection on lung CTs: Combining Computer Vision and Deep Learning with Python

One project I am especially proud of is the Covid-19 detector for lung CTs that I developed with Computer Vision and Deep Learning Tools in Python.

The official paper regarding this work will be published shortly. For now, take a look at the Detector output and its impressive accuracy of about 97%, which highly exceeds average radiologist performance:


The CT images are pre-processed for segmentation and feature enhancement unsing Python in combination with OpenCV.

The pre-processed images were then used to train a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) for the classification task using Pytorch.

Taking part at the public Covid-19 detection challenge at grand-challenge.org, the final model outperformed all candidates on the Leaderboard on the official test-set.

The final performance measurements for this task were:

  • Total Accuracy: 0.9704
  • F1-Score: 0.9697
  • AUC: 0.9707
